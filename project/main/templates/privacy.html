Políticas de Privacidade

A sua privacidade é importante para nós.
Afinal, a nossa equipe é composta por maníacos por controle e privacidade. Nossas Políticas de Privacidade definem quais informações coletamos, como usamos e como protegemos a sua privacidade, além dos seus direitos em relação a elas.

  1. Information We Collect

    We may collect the following information: Basic account details (e.g., name, email, username) Profile information (e.g., university, major, goals) Activity data (e.g., posts, suggestions, friendships) Login and session data (e.g., IP address, timestamps)

  2. How We Use Your Information

    We use your information to: Create and manage your account Facilitate academic and social interactions Moderate content and ensure safety Improve platform features and usability

  3. Data Sharing

    We do not share your personal information with third parties. We may disclose data only: When required by law or court order To investigate abuse, fraud, or misuse of the platform

  4. Cookies and Session Data

    We use cookies and similar technologies only to manage sessions (e.g., staying logged in). These are essential for the platform to function.

  5. Your Rights

    You have the right to: Access, update, or delete your personal data Request correction of incorrect information Withdraw from the platform at any time To exercise these rights, please contact the platform team.

  6. Data Security

    We take reasonable steps to secure your data, including encryption of sensitive fields and access control for moderators and administrators.

  7. Platform Scope

    This platform is a student project, not a commercial service. As such, it is not subject to commercial regulations like GDPR or CCPA, but we still value and respect your privacy.
